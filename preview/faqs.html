FAQs

Clear answers to the questions we hear most — so you can arrive, settle in, and enjoy Escondido.

General stay

What are your check-in and check-out times?

Check-in starts at 3:00 PM, and check-out is at 11:00 AM.

Is the front desk open 24/7?

Yes. Our front desk is available 24/7 for arrivals and guest support.

Is the property non-smoking?

Yes. Motel Mediteran is a smoke-free property.

Do I need a valid ID at check-in?

Yes. A valid government-issued photo ID is required at check-in.

Can I request early check-in or late check-out?

Requests may be available based on occupancy and housekeeping status. Please contact the front desk in advance.

Is there a minimum age to check in?

The minimum age to check in is 18.

Parking & location

Do you offer parking?

Yes. Complimentary on-site parking is available for all registered guests.

How far are you from downtown Escondido?

About 2 miles from the center of downtown Escondido.

How close is the San Diego Zoo Safari Park?

About a 10-15 minute drive, roughly 6 miles from the motel.

Rooms & amenities

Is WiFi included in the rooms?

Yes. Complimentary high-speed WiFi is available in all rooms and common areas.

What in-room amenities are included?

Rooms include premium bedding with pillowtop mattresses, climate control, flat-screen cable TV, a work desk, and daily housekeeping.

Do you have rooms with a kitchen or hot tub?

Select room types include kitchen setups and upgraded features, based on availability.

Do all rooms include a microwave and refrigerator?

Yes. All rooms include both a microwave and refrigerator.

Are accessible rooms available?

Yes. Mobility-accessible room types are available, but they are subject to availability and cannot be guaranteed. Request these room types when booking.

Do you have connecting rooms?

Connecting rooms are subject to availability and cannot be guaranteed.

Facilities

Do you have a pool?

Yes. We have an outdoor pool open daily, typically from 8:30 AM to 9:00 PM.

Is laundry available on-site?

Yes. Self-service laundry facilities are available on-site.

Is the pool heated?

No. The pool is not heated.

Do you have EV charging on-site?

We don’t have chargers on-site. Nearest public fast charging is usually about 10 minutes away at 110 W El Norte Pkwy, Escondido — confirm availability and plug type in your EV app before you go.

Policies

Do you allow pets?

Pets are not permitted; service animals are welcome.

Is a security deposit required?

A refundable security deposit may be required at check-in (typically around $100).

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, and cash.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations should be made at least 48 hours before 3:00 PM on the arrival date to avoid charges. Late changes may incur one night room and tax.

Dining

Is breakfast included with the stay?

A full breakfast isn’t part of the rate, but we offer complimentary coffee in the office. Plenty of breakfast spots are just minutes away if you want a hot meal.

Do you have an on-site restaurant?

No on-site restaurant, but you are minutes from local favorites, cafes, and casual dining options.

Transportation

Do you provide airport shuttle service?

No. Airport shuttle service is not currently available.

How far is the nearest airport from the motel?

McClellan-Palomar Airport is about a 30-minute drive away.

Guest experience

Is Motel Mediteran family-friendly?

Yes. Families are welcome, and children may stay using existing bedding.

What do guests love most about staying here?

Guests most often mention clean and comfortable rooms, friendly service, and a quiet, relaxed atmosphere.

Can the hotel receive packages for guests?

Package handling may be available for registered guests. Please call ahead with your delivery details before shipping.
