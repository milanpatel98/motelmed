Amenities

A relaxed North County stay—close to the Safari Park, wineries, and I‑15—with the essentials done right: free parking, Wi‑Fi, and a pool when you want to unwind.

We focus on affordability and a welcoming stay. Motel Mediteran is a quiet home base in Escondido, where you can enjoy a swim in our outdoor pool and easy access to the attractions that brought you to San Diego County. We aim to make families and solo travelers alike comfortable, with room options and services suited to short visits or longer stays.

For your enjoyment

On site, you’ll find an outdoor pool, complimentary Wi‑Fi, and a guest laundry—plus free parking, a 24-hour front desk, complimentary coffee in the office, and copy & fax when you need it.

Guest rooms are smoke-free and equipped with a pillowtop mattress, flat-screen TV, hairdryer, microwave, and refrigerator. Wake-up service is available, and we provide an iron and ironing board for your convenience.
