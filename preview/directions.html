Get directions

Motel Mediteran sits on South Escondido Boulevard—easy to reach from Interstate 15 and a short ride from downtown Escondido, the Safari Park, and North County wineries.

Whether you are driving in from San Diego, Orange County, or connecting from the Sprinter, use the overview below to plan your arrival. Tap the button to open turn-by-turn directions in Google Maps.

Start your journey

Property map

By car

From San Diego & the south

Take I-15 North toward Temecula. Near Escondido, use the 9th Avenue or Centre City Parkway exit and follow signs toward downtown. Turn onto South Escondido Boulevard and continue south—we are on the east side of the street.

From Riverside & the north

Take I-15 South into Escondido. Exit toward 9th Avenue or Centre City Parkway, then head south on South Escondido Boulevard to 2336.

From Orange County & Los Angeles

Take I-5 North to CA-78 East toward Escondido, or connect to I-15 North and follow the San Diego directions above. Allow extra time during coastal and weekend traffic.

From Ramona & the east

Take SR-78 West into Escondido. Exit toward South Escondido Boulevard or Centre City Parkway and continue south to the motel.

By trolley

NCTD Sprinter (light rail)

The Sprinter runs between Oceanside and Escondido. Get off at Escondido Transit Center (also called the Escondido Sprinter station). From there, take a Breeze bus that serves South Escondido Boulevard, or a short taxi or rideshare south to 2336 S. Escondido Blvd.

From the coast (Coaster + Sprinter)

Take the Coaster to Oceanside, transfer to the Sprinter toward Escondido, and exit at Escondido Transit Center. Plan connections on NCTD’s schedule—weekend and evening service is more limited.

Local buses

North County Breeze routes connect the transit center with major corridors. Check the current map for the line that best serves South Escondido Boulevard near your arrival time.
