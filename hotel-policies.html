Check-in & check-out
  • Check-in: From 3:00 PM
  • Check-out: By 11:00 AM
  • Front desk: Open 24/7
  • Minimum check-in age: 18 years old
  • Valid photo ID: Required at check-in
Payment & deposit
  • Accepted cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  • Cash: Accepted
  • Security deposit: $100 at check-in
  • Cancellation: Cancellations should be made at least 48 hours before 3:00 PM on the arrival date to avoid charges. Late changes may incur one night room and tax.
Property rules
  • Smoking: Non-smoking property
  • Pets: Not allowed (service animals welcome)
  • Visitor policy: Please ask at the front desk
Parking & transportation
  • Parking: Complimentary on-site self-parking
  • Airport shuttle: Not available
  • Nearest airport: San Diego International Airport (SAN) (about 45 minutes)
  • EV charging: Not on-site. Nearest public fast charging is usually about 10 minutes away at 110 W El Norte Pkwy, Escondido (multiple networks — check your EV app for live availability and plug type).
Room & stay details
  • WiFi: Complimentary in all rooms and common areas
  • Microwave + refrigerator: Included in all rooms
  • Accessible rooms: Subject to availability — request when booking
  • Connecting rooms: Subject to availability
Facilities
  • Pool: Outdoor pool (not heated)
  • Laundry: Self-service laundry on-site
  • Breakfast: A full breakfast isn’t included, but complimentary coffee is available in the office. Local cafés and restaurants are nearby if you’d like more.
  • On-site restaurant: Not available
