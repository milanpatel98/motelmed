For you and your family

Maximize fun and save money at Motel Mediteran, the closest motel to San Diego's Safari Park and centrally located near top attractions like SeaWorld, the San Diego Zoo, Legoland, and Disneyland. Beaches and outdoor adventures are just a short drive away.

For Business Travel

Stay productive at Motel Mediteran, conveniently located off I-15 in Escondido, with San Diego International Airport (SAN) about 35–40 minutes away by car. Enjoy free Wi-Fi, HBO, Showtime, and a business center to keep you on track. Smart, efficient, and comfortable—Motel Mediteran has you covered.
